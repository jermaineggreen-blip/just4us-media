Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

The short answers. Hit us on contact if you need more.

Do I need a credit card for the 24-Hour All-Access Pass?

No. The 24-Hour All-Access Pass is genuinely free — Whop will ask for your email to send you credentials, but no payment method is required to get started.

How fast will I get my login credentials?

Most activations take up to 24 hours — accounts are manually provisioned to keep the service stable. You'll receive your credentials and setup guide by email as soon as activation completes.

What devices does this work on?

Everything. Smart TVs, Firestick, Apple TV, Android boxes, MAG boxes, iPhone, Android, Windows, Mac, Linux — anything that runs an IPTV player like IPTV Smarters, TiviMate, VLC, or Kodi.

What happens after the 24 hours end?

Your trial expires automatically — nothing auto-charges. You'll get a reminder email with upgrade options so you can pick the plan that fits.

Can I stream on multiple devices at once?

Depends on your plan. 1-connection plans stream one device at a time. 2- and 3-connection plans let multiple family members watch different things simultaneously. See the plans page for details.

Do you offer refunds?

Because we give a free trial upfront, paid plans are non-refundable. That's exactly why we hand you the 24-Hour All-Access Pass first — test everything before you pay.
