Partner FAQ

What's the difference between Silver and Gold?
Silver partners earn 50% recurring commission with basic white-label branding. Gold partners earn 70% commission with full white-label rights, dedicated account manager, and priority support. Gold is best if you want to resell under your own brand.
When do I get paid?
You receive monthly payouts every month. As long as your customers remain active, you keep earning 50-70% of their monthly subscription. Payments are sent via bank transfer or PayPal.
Do I need technical skills?
No technical skills required. We provide everything: sales funnel, landing pages, checkout, customer support. Your job is just to send traffic. That's it.
Can I rebrand as my own service?
Yes, but only with Gold tier. Gold partners get full white-label rights—your logo, your domain, your branding on everything. Silver partners get basic white-label (your name on the funnel, but not full branding).
Is there a minimum number of customers?
No minimum. Earn on every single customer you bring in. Whether it's 1 customer or 1,000, you get your 50-70% commission forever.
What if a customer cancels?
You only earn commission while they're active. If they cancel, your commission stops for that customer. But you keep earning on all your other active customers.
Do you provide marketing materials?
Yes. We provide email swipes, social media templates, video clips, and other marketing assets. Gold partners get additional co-marketing opportunities and dedicated support for campaigns.
How do I track my customers and earnings?
Your partner dashboard tracks everything: active customers, monthly revenue, commissions earned, and detailed analytics. Real-time reporting so you always know your numbers.
